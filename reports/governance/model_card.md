# Model Card - Draft

## Model name
Canadian Retail Credit Default Early-Warning Model

## Model status
Draft; not trained yet.

## Intended use
Support portfolio risk monitoring and borrower-level risk review in a simulated Canadian retail lending context.

## Not intended for
Automated final lending decisions, adverse action decisions, production deployment, or regulatory capital estimation.

## Target
`Defaulter = 1`
